The Problem

Solo founders make decisions in a vacuum

  • No executive team. Early-stage founders lack access to specialized CFO, CMO, CTO, and legal counsel.
  • Generic Advice. Standard LLMs don't know your specific burn rate, active contracts, or OKRs. They guess.
  • Single-perspective AI. ChatGPT gives one answer. Real decisions need adversarial debate — tension surfaces blind spots.
  • Slow, expensive counsel. Hiring advisors costs thousands and takes weeks. Strategy decisions can't wait.
The Solution

An AI board that knows your business

Quorum assembles four domain-expert AI executives who analyze your question in parallel, referencing your real-time company data.

Ingenuity

Secure, Siloed, & Context-Aware

Data privacy meets hyper-personalization. Each agent is a siloed entity that accesses only the data it needs to do its job.

1

Siloed Execution

Agents run in strict isolation. The CMO agent cannot access raw payroll data; the CFO cannot see unreleased code. This "Zero-Trust" architecture ensures enterprise-grade security & compliance.

2

Drive Context Injector

We connect to your Google Drive via RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation). When you ask about "Project X," agents pull the actual PDFs and Sheets for Project X.

3

Adversarial Synthesis

The "Board Secretary" synthesizes the conflict. It sees the arguments, not the raw source documents, adding another layer of data safety.
